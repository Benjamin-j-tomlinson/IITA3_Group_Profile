Michael Reyland

General Manager – Renewable Energy

The job is a general manager position, the technical and general skills needed for the position is broad in scope with a degree in engineering, commerce, economics or finance and experience in construction or project management. Aspects of the ideal job are; leadership is required for the role being a general management position overseeing; finances; human resources; project development, commercial and technical functions; sales to generate growth and maintain relationships with new or existing clientele.

General managers are below CEO and executives but are above many of the employees. In industry general managers oversee operations of department heads through delegation and direction. A general manager explicitly presides over hiring, coaching, finance and administrative functions. A general manager is also obligated to improve on business functions including implementing revised strategies and policies aligned to the objectives of the organisation.

Grant Dawson

Service Owner - Telecommunications

The job position is in telecommunications as a contract holder overseeing field-based deployments and support. The job requires a degree in information technology with ITIL, networking certifications and project management expertise. The position entails management of; vendors with corresponding service level agreements; technical leadership; root cause analysis and escalation; performance programs; communication with involved stakeholders and overseeing changes and integration of ICT projects.

Service owners are accountable for deliverable IT services, this includes the management of the of the service. The service owner also is the representative of the company and must have knowledge of the service and the organisation they are responsible to represent. They are also required to attend regular SLA (Service level agreement) and OLA (Operational level agreement) meetings. The service owner must constantly improve upon the service in all aspects. The service owner is the general manager of the service.

In terms of timing, my qualifications, technical and commercial skills and work experience should all line up nicely in around 5-7 years if I stick to this plan.

This job requires a Bachelor of software engineering or equivalent, expertise in Java, object-oriented programming, kotlin, iOS, SQL and JIRA. The job requires the development and maintenance of the Tinybeans application, working closely with the engineering and design team to meet customer needs and criterion.

Android developers are involved with developing sophisticated android applications. Typically, developers create, test and fix the code for the platform. Experience is normally required in android app development and expertise in mobile application development. Knowledge of databases, APIs, software development lifecycle and back-end technologies are required. From junior level, developers move to intermediate level for more complex projects. After the intermediate developer level, senior development is the next phase in which they report to a tech lead, supervise and support other developers.
